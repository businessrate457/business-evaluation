How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to:
businessrate457@gmail.com

Your email should include:

  • Your registered email address used in the app
  • Your full name
  • A brief request stating that you want your account and data deleted

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your user profile and login credentials
  • Any business evaluations or reviews you submitted
  • Associated analytics or usage history

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain certain non-personal data or legally required information for the following reasons:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud or abuse
  • To maintain internal records of prior deletion requests
